About

The intersection of engineering and design

While my background in Electrical Engineering is built on mathematics and logic, my perspective is defined by an appreciation for the arts. Whether it's the classical form of Michelangelo's David or the visual storytelling of 2001: A Space Odyssey, I am fascinated by work that marries structure with beauty. I channel this duality into my own photography and web design, striving to create work that is as technically sound as it is visually compelling.

My work exists at the intersection where engineered logic meets human creativity.
